I write reproducible papers with solutions to real-world problems

Postdoc at Amazon on MLLMs. I did my PhD on computer vision for localization at Polytechnic of Turin with Prof. Barbara Caputo and Prof. Carlo Masone, with a visiting at Carnegie Mellon University with Prof. Sebastian Scherer. Before my PhD I worked at the Italian Institute of Technology.
TLDR: Computer Vision PhD with 8 first-author CVPR / ICCV / ECCV papers, over 2000 GitHub stars
